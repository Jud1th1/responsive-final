About RUSH

Our Vision:

Rise Up Serve Haiti Inc. vision is to promote higher education and community empowerment.


Our Mission:

Rise Up Serve Haiti's Mission is to educate and empower the Haitian community ( men, women, and children) in Marin, Bertrand, and Tricon using sustainable approaches to development by providing economic, cultural, health, and social services support.
