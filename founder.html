Meet the Founder

Minadia Phurcien


Minadia Phurcien founded Rise Up Serve Haiti in March of 2017 with the vision and dedication have address important issues and make a positive impact in the lives of those in Haiti. As a descendant of Haiti, Minadia has always held an abundance of love of the country in her heart and dreams of making a difference. Through the hard work of our team and the generosity of our supporters, we have been able to make a meaningful difference in our community. Now returning from a brief hiatus, we invite you to learn more about our organization, our mission, and the work we do. Together, we can create a better world.
